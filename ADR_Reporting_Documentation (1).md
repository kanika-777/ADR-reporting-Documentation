# Case Scenario

**Patient Name:** Rajesh Kumar  
**Age:** 45 years  
**Gender:** Male  
**Weight:** 70 kg  

**Medical History:** Hypertension

**Suspected Medicine:** Amoxicillin 500 mg orally, three times daily

**Indication:** Respiratory Tract Infection

**Reaction Observed:** Generalized skin rash with itching after two days of therapy.

**Date of Onset:** 10 July 2026

**Action Taken:** Medicine discontinued.

**Treatment Given:** Cetirizine 10 mg once daily.

**Outcome:** Patient recovered within four days.

**Reporter:** Clinical Pharmacist
